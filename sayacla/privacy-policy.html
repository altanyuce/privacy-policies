Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the Sayaçla mobile application developed by Altan Yüce. Sayaçla is designed to work without requiring a user account, sign-in, or the collection of personal data.

Information We Collect

Sayaçla does not collect, request, transmit, sell, or share personal data. Users are not required to create an account or sign in to use the application.

How Data Is Used

Because the application does not collect personal data, no personal data is used for profiling, advertising, analytics, or any other purpose.

Local Storage

Application data, such as counters and user preferences, may be stored locally on the user's device. This data remains on the device and is not sent to the developer or to external servers. An internet connection is not required for the basic use of the application.

Permissions

Sayaçla does not use location, camera, microphone, or contacts. The application does not require access to these sensitive device features for its core functionality.

  • No location access is used.
  • No camera access is used.
  • No microphone access is used.
  • No contacts access is used.

Third-Party Services

Sayaçla does not use advertising SDKs and does not use analytics services. No third-party service is used to collect or process personal data through the application.

Children's Privacy

Sayaçla does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since the application does not collect personal data, it does not knowingly process children's personal information.

Data Security

The application is designed to keep its data locally on the user's device. Users are responsible for the security of their own device, including device backups, operating system protections, and physical access.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated when application functionality or legal requirements change. Any updates will be published on this page with a revised last updated date.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact the developer at altanyuce160@gmail.com.

Gizlilik Politikası

Bu Gizlilik Politikası, Altan Yüce tarafından geliştirilen Sayaçla mobil uygulaması için geçerlidir. Sayaçla, kullanıcı hesabı, giriş işlemi veya kişisel veri toplanması gerektirmeyecek şekilde tasarlanmıştır.

Toplanan Veriler

Sayaçla kişisel veri toplamaz, talep etmez, iletmez, satmaz veya paylaşmaz. Uygulamayı kullanmak için kullanıcı hesabı oluşturulması ya da giriş yapılması gerekmez.

Verilerin Kullanımı

Uygulama kişisel veri toplamadığı için kişisel veriler profilleme, reklam, analiz veya başka bir amaçla kullanılmaz.

Yerel Depolama

  • Kamera erişimi kullanılmaz.
  • Mikrofon erişimi kullanılmaz.
  • Rehber erişimi kullanılmaz.

Üçüncü Taraf Hizmetler
